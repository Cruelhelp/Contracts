Equity Allocation Agreement

THIS EQUITY ALLOCATION AGREEMENT is made and entered into as of the date of last signature below (the "Effective Date"), by and among the undersigned parties (collectively, the "Parties").

Article I: Parties

The Parties to this Agreement are as follows:

  • Founder: Ruel McNeil (hereinafter referred to as the "Founder")
  • Team Member 1: Ineiko McBean
  • Team Member 2: Shaquille Simpson
  • Team Member 3: Darren Downer

Article II: Recitals

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to establish and formalize the allocation of membership interests in GoatMouth, a Limited Liability Company (the "Company");

WHEREAS, the Parties acknowledge their respective contributions and commitments to the success and growth of the Company;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties agree as follows:

Article III: Equity Allocation

The Parties agree to the following allocation of membership interests in the Company:

  1. Founder's Interest: The Founder shall retain fifty percent (50%) of the Company's membership interests, representing the Founder's initial contribution, vision, and leadership role in establishing the Company.
  2. Initial Team Member Interests: Each of the three Team Members (Ineiko McBean, Shaquille Simpson, and Darren Downer) shall initially receive ten percent (10%) of the Company's membership interests upon making their first significant contribution to the Company. Such contributions may include, but are not limited to:
    • Completion of an initial assigned task or project;
    • Delivery of functional code or technical work product;
    • Making a financial investment in the Company;
    • Any other milestone mutually agreed upon by the Parties.
  3. Contribution Pool: The remaining twenty percent (20%) of membership interests shall constitute a contribution pool (the "Pool"). The Pool shall be allocated over time based on additional contributions by any Party, including but not limited to:
    • Financial investments;
    • Development work and technical contributions;
    • Business development activities;
    • Marketing and promotional efforts;
    • Other significant efforts that add demonstrable value to the Company.
  4. Pool Allocation Process: Allocations from the Pool shall be determined by mutual agreement of the Parties holding majority interests at the time of such allocation. All allocations shall be made with a focus on fairness, transparency, and proportionality to the value contributed.

Article IV: Additional Terms and Conditions

  1. Operating Agreement: All membership interests allocated pursuant to this Agreement shall remain subject to the terms of the Company's Operating Agreement, to be executed separately by all members.
  2. Vesting Schedule: The Parties acknowledge that vesting schedules and conditions may be established in the formal Operating Agreement to align incentives and ensure long-term commitment.
  3. Preliminary Nature: This document constitutes a preliminary agreement regarding equity distribution and shall be superseded by formal legal documentation prepared by qualified legal counsel.
  4. Good Faith Cooperation: All Parties commit to acting in good faith, with honesty, transparency, and mutual respect, and to collaborating diligently toward the success and growth of the Company.
  5. Amendment: This Agreement may be amended only by written instrument executed by all Parties.
  6. Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws applicable to the Company's jurisdiction of formation.

IMPORTANT NOTICE: This document does not constitute formal legal advice and has not been prepared by an attorney. The Parties are strongly encouraged to consult with qualified legal counsel to prepare binding legal documents, including a comprehensive LLC Operating Agreement, appropriate to their jurisdiction and specific circumstances. The Parties acknowledge that they have had the opportunity to seek independent legal advice before executing this Agreement.
